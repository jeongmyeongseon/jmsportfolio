Nature Story

It is not clear when the word "nature" was used, but it is already used in many places in the Tao Da Sutra. The meaning of nature expressed in the Tao Da sutra refers to things that have existed since the beginning in response to human society, or the order of the universe. Unlike nature in modern languages, the nature in the Daedokyeong is not a noun, but it originally means that it is itself. For example, there is a saying in the Commentary on the Taoji-gyeong, which means that heaven and earth are nature in modern language, and nature is what it is.
